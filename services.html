Our Services

At WTPMS, we offer much more than just medicine. Our services are designed to educate, support, and empower patients and caregivers across all ages.

  • Basic health consultations and patient support
  • Blood pressure and blood sugar monitoring
  • Medication advice and side-effect education
  • Product sourcing for rare or specialty medications
  • Local and regional delivery of supplies
  • Vaccination guidance and scheduling support

We also partner with healthcare organizations for outreach campaigns and mobile support.

Our trained personnel are available to offer tailored care for elderly patients and expectant mothers.

We are adding digital services to support e-consultations and prescription uploads.

Every service is delivered with confidentiality, respect, and cultural awareness.

WTPMS is your one-stop destination for holistic, ethical healthcare service delivery.
