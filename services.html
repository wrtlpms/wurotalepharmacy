Our Services

At Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS), we are proud to provide a wide range of pharmaceutical services designed to support the health and well-being of our community. Below is an overview of our key services:

Dispensing of Prescription & OTC Medications

We provide a wide selection of both prescription and over-the-counter (OTC) medicines, strictly following pharmaceutical standards and regulations approved by NAFDAC and NAPPMED.

Pharmaceutical Counseling

Our experienced team offers patient education and advice on drug usage, side effects, dosage schedules, and drug interactions to ensure proper treatment outcomes.

Blood Pressure & Blood Sugar Monitoring

We provide quick and accurate monitoring for blood pressure and blood sugar levels, helping customers manage chronic conditions like hypertension and diabetes.

First Aid & Medical Supplies

From bandages and antiseptics to surgical gloves and diagnostic tools, we stock various medical supplies for homes, clinics, and institutions.

Referral and Health Support

We assist customers by connecting them to hospitals, specialists, or testing centers when necessary. We are committed to bridging the gap between pharmacy and healthcare.

Home Delivery (Coming Soon)

WTPMS is working on launching a home delivery service for drugs and medical essentials to improve convenience for our customers.

Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of professionalism and care in all the services we offer at Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS).
