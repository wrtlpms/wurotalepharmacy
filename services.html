Services

At Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS), we offer a wide range of pharmaceutical and medical services tailored to meet the healthcare needs of individuals, families, and institutions. Our commitment to excellence ensures that each service we provide is grounded in professionalism, safety, and compassion.

Our core services include the dispensing of prescription and over-the-counter medications. We maintain a comprehensive inventory of trusted brands and generic alternatives, all sourced from verified manufacturers and compliant with regulatory standards set by the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC).

WTPMS also offers counseling services to ensure proper medication use. Our trained professionals take the time to educate customers about dosage, potential side effects, and drug interactions, especially for chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma.

As a registered member of the National Association of Patent and Proprietary Medicine Dealers (NAPPMED), we adhere strictly to guidelines on the safe distribution of pharmaceutical products. This affiliation reinforces our credibility and guarantees that our services align with industry standards.

In addition to medicine supply, we offer hospital-grade medical equipment such as blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, orthopedic supports, and wound care items. We also provide surgical supplies for clinics and small-scale health centers in need of trusted procurement.

Our inventory extends to wellness and personal care products, including supplements, vitamins, baby care items, skin care essentials, and hygiene products. We cater to both retail and wholesale customers and are fully equipped to handle large-volume orders for hospitals, NGOs, and government programs.

We support patients through follow-up reminders, refill notifications, and in-store consultations. WTPMS is also proud to partner with healthcare professionals to improve prescription adherence and treatment outcomes across our community.

Our services are continually evolving to meet the changing needs of the health sector. Whether you walk into our store or contact us online, you can be assured of reliable service, quality products, and genuine concern for your well-being.

Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS) is more than just a pharmacy — we are a community healthcare partner committed to advancing access to quality healthcare for all.
