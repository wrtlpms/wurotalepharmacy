About Us

Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies was founded with a deep commitment to improving the quality of healthcare in our community. Based in Gabukka Quarters, Gombe, we operate as both a retail and wholesale outlet for a wide range of pharmaceutical products and medical supplies. Our goal is to bridge the gap in access to genuine medications and basic healthcare support for individuals, families, and institutions.

We believe in professionalism, integrity, and excellence. Our experienced staff is always ready to offer guidance on medication use, health advice, and access to products that promote wellness. At Wuro Tale, we strive to build lasting relationships with our customers, ensuring their health and satisfaction come first. We also offer sourcing and delivery services to support hospitals, clinics, and healthcare workers across the region.
