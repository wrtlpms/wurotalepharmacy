Welcome to Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS)

We are a trusted pharmacy and medical supply store located in Gombe, Nigeria — dedicated to delivering quality health products, pharmaceutical care, and professional services to our community.

At WTPMS, we believe healthcare should be accessible, safe, and personal. Whether you're visiting us for medications, health advice, or essential supplies, our team is here to support you with care and professionalism.

Medical Equipment

From thermometers to blood pressure monitors, we provide reliable health tools for clinics and households.

Community Trust

Our integrity and consistency have earned us a solid reputation among residents of Gombe and neighboring areas.

Professional Advice

Our knowledgeable staff provides counseling and support to help you understand your medications and health choices.
