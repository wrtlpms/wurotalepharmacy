Welcome to Wuro Tale Pharmacy

We are a trusted healthcare provider located in Gombe, Nigeria. At Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS), we are dedicated to delivering affordable, safe, and effective medical and pharmaceutical products to individuals and institutions alike.

Whether you need prescription medications, over-the-counter remedies, or medical equipment, our comprehensive inventory is here to support your needs.

Our mission is to bridge the healthcare gap in underserved communities through professionalism and care.

We maintain high standards in product sourcing and ensure that our supplies meet both national and international regulations.

Our team is comprised of certified pharmacists, health advisors, and trained professionals who offer free consultations and guidance on medication use.

At WTPMS, we don’t just dispense medicine—we build relationships and foster wellness with every service we offer.

We are expanding into digital health platforms to improve access and support across the region.

Visit us at Gabukka Quarters or reach out to us through WhatsApp or Facebook for fast support.

Stay connected as we grow our gallery and add even more services to make healthcare easy, personal, and available to all.
