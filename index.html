Welcome to Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS), where your health and wellness come first. We are a professional and customer-focused pharmacy dedicated to delivering quality pharmaceutical products, reliable medical supplies, and expert advice to our clients. Located in the heart of Gabukka Quarters, Gombe, WTPMS stands as a beacon of trusted healthcare solutions for individuals, families, and institutions alike.

At WTPMS, we understand the importance of access to safe, effective, and affordable medications. That’s why we stock a wide range of approved medicines, healthcare products, supplements, and wellness items. Whether you need over-the-counter drugs, prescription medicines, or personal medical equipment, we have you covered.

Our facility is operated by certified professionals who are passionate about health education, proper drug usage, and ensuring our clients receive the best care possible. We strive to bridge the gap between healthcare providers and patients by offering timely, accurate, and supportive services.

We are proud to serve both walk-in customers and healthcare institutions, maintaining the highest standards in storage, hygiene, and customer service. Every product is sourced from reputable manufacturers and stored under strict conditions to ensure quality.

Our vision is to be the leading pharmacy in the region by continually innovating, educating, and expanding access to essential medical supplies. Whether you are managing a chronic condition, recovering from illness, or simply looking to maintain a healthy lifestyle, WTPMS is your reliable partner.
