About Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS)

Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS) is a community-based pharmaceutical and medical supply business located in Gombe, Nigeria. We are dedicated to improving public health by offering high-quality, safe, and affordable medications, as well as essential medical supplies for individuals, families, and healthcare providers.

Founded by Comr. Muhammad Bello Shehu, a BSc Biology graduate from Gombe State University, WTPMS has grown from a humble local pharmacy into a trusted provider of pharmaceutical care and health-related products. Our founder brings a deep understanding of biology, wellness, and patient care, which shapes our day-to-day services and customer support.

At WTPMS, we are committed to professional ethics and compliance with all Nigerian regulatory bodies, including NAFDAC (National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control) and NAPPMED (National Association of Patent and Proprietary Medicine Dealers). We ensure that every product we sell and every service we provide aligns with approved health and safety guidelines.

Our team is composed of trained staff who provide expert advice and assistance on drug usage, proper dosage, and managing chronic conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, and malaria. We are customer-focused and constantly updating our inventory to meet the evolving health needs of the community.

WTPMS also emphasizes health education and outreach, offering guidance on drug compliance, side effects, and lifestyle choices. Our goal is not just to sell medicine but to become a reliable partner in your health journey.

Our commitment to quality, care, and trust has earned us a positive reputation among our customers, both locally and beyond. We take pride in being approachable, professional, and always ready to help.

Visit us at our store in Gabukka Quarters, Opposite Izalah Juma’at Mosque, Gombe, or contact us online. We’re here to serve you better, every day.
