About Wuro Tale Pharmacy

WTPMS was founded to revolutionize community healthcare in Gombe and neighboring states.

We believe that everyone deserves access to trustworthy, high-quality healthcare—regardless of income, education, or geography.

Our business is driven by values of integrity, compassion, and professionalism.

We serve walk-in patients, health workers, hospitals, and organizations with retail and bulk medical supplies.

With a physical location in Gabukka Quarters, we’re deeply integrated with the local community, understanding their needs and challenges.

From medication counseling to follow-ups, we go beyond basic pharmacy services.

We understand the risks of counterfeit drugs in the market—that's why we maintain verified supply chains and direct manufacturer partnerships.

WTPMS also supports local clinics through sourcing partnerships and educational materials.

As we scale, we are integrating more digital tools to reach patients even in remote areas.

We participate in healthcare awareness campaigns, vaccination drives, and maternal health initiatives.

At our core, we are passionate about improving lives through trusted medical support.

We see every customer not just as a patient, but as a member of our growing healthcare family.

Thank you for trusting us with your health and well-being—we’re proud to serve you.
