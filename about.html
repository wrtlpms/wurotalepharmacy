About

Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS) was established with a mission to provide high-quality healthcare products and professional pharmaceutical services to individuals and institutions in Gombe State and beyond. From the outset, our goal has been simple yet ambitious: to become a trusted name in the delivery of safe, reliable, and affordable medical solutions for the community we serve.

At WTPMS, we believe that access to good health should not be a privilege but a basic right. Our operations are founded on ethical practices, community engagement, and a deep commitment to the well-being of our clients. Every customer is treated with respect, transparency, and care because we understand that behind every prescription is a person who depends on us.

Our pharmacy is staffed with experienced professionals who bring knowledge, empathy, and integrity to their work. We invest in continuous training and development to ensure our team remains current with the latest pharmaceutical standards and healthcare trends. Our staff is always available to provide personalized advice and assist in selecting the right medications or supplies.

Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies (WTPMS) operates in full compliance with the regulatory guidelines of the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC) and the National Association of Patent and Proprietary Medicine Dealers (NAPPMED). This ensures that all our products meet safety, efficacy, and quality standards as approved by Nigeria’s healthcare authorities.

We maintain strict standards in procurement, storage, and distribution of all medicines and medical supplies. Our adherence to national regulatory requirements reflects our ongoing commitment to public safety and professional responsibility.

Over the years, WTPMS has grown not only in size but in reputation. We have expanded our offerings to include a wide variety of pharmaceuticals, hospital-grade supplies, wellness products, and consultation services—all aimed at improving lives.

The values that drive us include compassion, professionalism, accountability, and a strong desire to make a positive difference in the health outcomes of our customers. We proudly serve families, hospitals, clinics, and public institutions, always prioritizing quality and safety.

As we continue to evolve, our vision remains unchanged: to be the most respected and dependable pharmacy and medical supplier in the region. With the trust of our community and a dedicated team, WTPMS is positioned to lead the way in modern, patient-centered healthcare delivery.
