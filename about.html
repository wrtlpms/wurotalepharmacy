About Us

Wuro Tale Pharmacy and Medical Supplies was established with the goal of improving healthcare accessibility in Gombe State. Our mission is to provide safe, affordable, and effective medications and medical supplies to the community. We are located in Gabukka Quarters and serve both retail and wholesale clients.

We specialize in prescription and over-the-counter medications, basic medical devices, first aid kits, and healthcare consultation. Our goal is to ensure that every household has access to the medications and health support they need for a healthier life.

We are driven by our passion for community wellness and a belief that everyone deserves quality healthcare support, no matter where they live.
